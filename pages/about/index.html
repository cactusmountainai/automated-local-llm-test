About Me

Hello! I'm Lane Smith, a passionate developer and creative problem solver based in San Francisco, CA. With over 5 years of experience in web development, I specialize in building beautiful, functional, and user-friendly digital experiences.

My journey in technology started when I built my first website at age 16. Since then, I've worked with startups and established companies alike, learning from some of the brightest minds in the industry. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and tackle challenging problems that push the boundaries of what's possible.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new coffee shops, hiking in the Bay Area, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I believe in work-life balance and think the best ideas come from a mix of focused work and creative downtime.

I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations. If you'd like to connect or discuss a potential project, feel free to reach out through my contact page!
